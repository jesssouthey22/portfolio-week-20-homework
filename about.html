Work -

Jess Southey is a Melbourne-based multi-disciplinary creative. After working as a freelance Artist, Illustrator and Graphic Designer since 2016, Jess has now added UX/UI designer to her long list of creative passions.

What I love about UX/UI:

  • User experience and Interaction design has filled a space between her two passions of Psychology and Design
  • Creating designs with purpose that are backed by research
  • Creative Problem Solving that has real-world meaning
  • Creating simple solutions to big problems.

    Life -

    Outside of work, I spend my time absorbing all things creative; painting, drawing, knitting, crafting and creating arworks for exhibitions. I’m highly sentimental and nostalgic and enjoy finding ways to connect with my childhood homes of South Africa, US and Australia through my personal work. When I’m not making something, I’ll be watching and discussing movies, tv and music with my partner, building LEGO, collecting figures and artwork and rearranging our displays at home.
